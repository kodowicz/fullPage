Looking for anything about plants? Find out something about us.

rosliny project / latin plantae / is the tandem of people and this what is growing from the soil. The dreams and the actions. In move and without the move. The art and the non art. We believe that the usually things can be unusually. This project’s purpose is to expand the idea it is worth appreciate even the smallest roots which after the passing time are sprouting strong and healthy.

Our planty goals

Until recently the potted plant was associated with a wilting poor fern at windowsill in staircase. Nowadays plants proudly present themself in fashionable resteurants, good designed interiors and increasingly in our houses. Creating home jungle is great idea to fight accumulated stress and test your responsebility for... living nature.

Our mission is to make the experience of discovering the perfect potted plants as wonderful as the plants themselves. We would love to bridge the gap between plants and people, offering products (plants, pots) and specially help for better understanding plant’s needs. We writed a book specially for these who always forget what your pupils need exept a huge love from you. In this book except for presenting design tricks with plants we would love to show people plants are a living independent existence that needs attention and love.
